About Us

Over fifty years serving the community

Condr Driving and Traffic School was established in 1973 by the master instructor, Jose Vargas. Since then, we have been family-owned and operated in Eagle Rock on Eagle Rock Blvd.

Our school likes to give our clients the knowledge and skills to obtain and maintain a driver license. We have always strived to teach good defensive driving skills to maintain safe driving practices. We give teen drivers a sound base to begin their driving future. We also help with the renewal or reissue of a driver license.

Condor driving school exclusivly gives private lessons. We thrieve for an amazing customer experience and focus 100% of our attention on your needs.
